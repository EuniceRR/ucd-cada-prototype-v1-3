CA Triggers:

  1. Application submitted

    Submitting the c100. No in progress version, only blank or fully ticked

    Cafcass child safety checks

    In progress - Cafcass informed of the case?

    Fully ticked - Cafcass letter uploaded to CCD

    Response submitted

    Different for applicants and respondents:

    Applicants - No in progress version for this, Fully ticked - court receives C7

    Respondents - Blank - no action, In progress - user clicks on 'Respond to the application', Fully ticked - C7 submitted

    Hearings and court orders

    In progress - first hearing/order is issued, Fully ticked - final order

    Case closed

    Fully ticked - final order
