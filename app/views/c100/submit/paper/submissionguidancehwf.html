The court will not receive your application until you complete these steps.

1. Download your application

2. Print your application and any attached documents

Print 3 copies of your entire application on A4 paper, single sided. The court will send one copy to the other people in the case, keep a copy, and return one copy to you.

Any attached documents must be included in full as part of each copy of the application.

3. Post it to the court

Post all 3 printed copies of your application to:
